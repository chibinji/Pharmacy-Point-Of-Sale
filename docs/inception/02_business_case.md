## 2. Business Objectives

| Objective | Target | Success Metric |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| **Reduce checkout time** | 50% reduction | Average transaction < 2 minutes |
| **Eliminate manual inventory** | 100% automation | Zero manual stock counts needed |
| **Prevent expired sales** | Zero incidents | No sales of expired medication |
| **Improve prescription accuracy** | 100% | Zero data entry errors |
| **Increase customer satisfaction** | 20% improvement | Customer survey scores |

## 3. Options Analysis

### Option 1: Do Nothing
| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|-----------|
| **Cost** | $0 upfront, but ongoing losses |
| **Benefits** | None |
| **Risks** | Continued inefficiencies, lost revenue, compliance risks |
| **Verdict** | ❌ Not recommended |

### Option 2: Buy Off-the-Shelf POS
| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|-----------|
| **Cost** | $X,XXX - $XX,XXX + monthly fees |
| **Benefits** | Quick deployment, proven software |
| **Risks** | May not handle pharmacy-specific needs, limited customization |
| **Verdict** | ⚠ Possible but may not meet all needs |

### Option 3: Build Custom System (Recommended)
| Aspect | Assessment |
|--------|-----------|
| **Cost** | Development effort (15 weeks) |
| **Benefits** | Tailored to pharmacy needs, full control, no ongoing license fees |
| **Risks** | Development timeline, technical challenges |
| **Verdict** | ✅ Recommended - best long-term value |

## 6. Assumptions & Constraints

### Assumptions
- Pharmacy staff will be available for testing and feedback
- Hardware (scanners, printers) is compatible with new system
- Internet connectivity is reliable
- Team has necessary skills to complete project

### Constraints
- 15-week academic timeline
- Must follow Unified Process methodology
- Must comply with pharmacy regulations
- Budget limited to free/open-source tools
