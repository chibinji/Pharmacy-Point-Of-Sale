## 1. Current Situation

[Pharmacy Name] is a community pharmacy currently operating with:
- **Manual cash register** with no integration to inventory
- **Paper-based prescription records** stored in filing cabinets
- **Hand-counted inventory** with periodic physical counts
- **Separate systems** for sales, prescriptions, and inventory

## 2. Identified Problems

### 2.1 Operational Inefficiencies
- **Slow checkout:** Average transaction time of 5-7 minutes
- **Manual inventory tracking:** Takes 4 hours weekly to count stock
- **Prescription lookup:** Takes 3-5 minutes to find patient history
- **Duplicate data entry:** Same information entered in multiple places

### 2.2 Business Risks
- **Expired medications:** No automated alerts for expiring stock
- **Stockouts:** Running out of critical medications
- **Overstock:** Wasted capital on slow-moving items
- **Prescription errors:** Manual data entry risks

### 2.3 Customer Experience Issues
- **Long wait times:** Especially during peak hours
- **No refill reminders:** Customers forget to refill medications
- **Insurance confusion:** Unclear coverage at checkout
- **No prescription status updates:** Customers don't know when ready

### 2.4 Compliance Concerns
- **Record keeping:** Manual logs for controlled substances
- **Audit preparation:** Time-consuming report generation
- **Regulatory changes:** Difficult to update manual processes

## 3. Impact of Problems

| Problem Area | Current Impact |
|--------------|----------------|
| **Revenue Loss** | Estimated 15% lost sales due to stockouts |
| **Labor Cost** | 20 hours/week spent on manual inventory |
| **Customer Dissatisfaction** | 3 complaints/week about wait times |
| **Compliance Risk** | Potential fines for record-keeping errors |

## 4. Opportunity

A dedicated Pharmacy Point of Sale system would:
- **Reduce transaction time** to under 2 minutes
- **Eliminate manual inventory** with real-time tracking
- **Prevent expired sales** with automatic alerts
- **Improve compliance** with digital audit trails
- **Enhance customer experience** with refill reminders

## 5. Stakeholders Affected

| Stakeholder | How They Are Affected |
|-------------|----------------------|
| **Pharmacists** | Spend 30% of time on paperwork instead of patient care |
| **Cashiers** | Frustrated by slow, manual process |
| **Customers** | Experience long wait times and service delays |
| **Manager** | Lacks visibility into inventory and sales trends |
| **Owner** | Losing revenue due to inefficiencies |
